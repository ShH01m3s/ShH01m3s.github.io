A different kind of therapy

Psychodynamic therapy doesn't offer quick fixes or generic coping tips. Instead, we explore the deeper patterns — unconscious forces, past experiences, relational dynamics — that quietly shape how you feel and act today. I also offer clinical hypnosis as an optional complement. It's a focused, collaborative process — nothing like what you've seen on TV — and we'd only explore it together if you feel it's right for you.

Deeper, open-ended exploration

Clinical hypnosis

Therapeutic hypnosis for anxiety, stress, habits, and emotional blocks. Entirely optional — can complement psychotherapy or work as a standalone approach.
