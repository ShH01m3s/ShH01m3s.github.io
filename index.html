About me

Currently completing my Master of Counselling (AIPC) and studies in Clinical Hypnotherapy (Clover Institute), I offer a supportive space for individuals to navigate their emotional journeys. My practice is built on a foundation of empathy, ensuring you feel understood, while also drawing on psychodynamic and CBT insights to help you challenge and reframe your thought patterns. I am a student member of both the Australian Counselling Association (ACA) and the Australian Society of Clinical Hypnotherapists (ASCH), with my graduation anticipated for late 2025.
