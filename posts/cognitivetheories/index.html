There are several cognitive-behavioural theories in psychotherapy. The first ever developed theory was REBT (Rational Emotive Behavioural Therapy). Although there are differences between REBT and general CBT, they largely rely on the same ideas and utilise the same techniques to alleviate unpleasant feelings.

Before diving deeper into each idea of CBT and the techniques used, I will leave this helpful list of possible tools to use to allay common and sometimes abhorrent disturbances:

  1. Mindfulness. If you are familiar with Buddhism, you might have already heard of this technique. You can find various mediation techniques and choose the one that suits your needs and character the best. No single technique is helpful to everyone.
