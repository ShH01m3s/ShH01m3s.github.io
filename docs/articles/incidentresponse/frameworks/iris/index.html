Cyberattack stages

  1. Attacker determines his objectives and prepared for the attack. Recon, prepare the tools, choose the tactics, prepare attack infrastucture. As a defender, make a list of the most probable targets, what is the most desired thing for them, what are the most likely attack vectors? Purchase all the domains that are the most likely targets.
  2. Launch and execute the attack. Defense tips: patch management, harden the attack surface, least priv, strong endpoint detection and mitigation strategies, employ a threat-hunting program.
  3. Attack continues. Expand access, escalate, move laterally, internal recon, persist. Defense tips: refine the threat-hunting program, central logging with prioritisation, whitelists and baselines, frequency analysis, enforce strong user password policies, 2FA etc.
  4. Attack gains strength. Tries to avoid detection. Defensice tips: analyse traffic and look for anomalies all the time, set up a honeypot.Monitor and inspect unsual data transfers (i.e. rar archives sent, emails to the outside world etc).
  5. Completes the mission. Defense tips: dedicated team to deal with the incidents, practice scenarios, forensics, use some tools like Resilient or others to centralise and automate.
