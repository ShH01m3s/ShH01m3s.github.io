Point of Sale Breach 💳

domain: ir

doctype: article


The main purpose is to steal the 16-digit card number 💳. Restraunts, retail, frocey are the most affected. 60% of transactions are via 💳.

PoS - technology used by a consumer to provide their payment information in exchange for a good or service. Since 1879. In the mid 70s was converted from mechanical to electronical form.

PCI DSS is the main card industry security standard. Created by JCB, Visa, MC, Discover Financial Services, AE to protect card holders.

Semi-integrated approach. Sensitive card data is isolated, encrypted and sent directly to the gateway. This way the payment or card data never touches the PoS. Another way is just secure the transport mechanism. Also, tokens instead of real data can be used. Once tokenised it can only be decoded by the processor.

PoS usually run on Windows rainbow 🌈 or Linux 🐧 software (in fact they are just small PCs).

Malware

Most of the malware is equipped with a backdoor and C&C features. The data of the card holder is decrypted in RAM, that’s why this type of malware sniffs the RAM (RAM scraping).

PoS Prevention

  • Monitor network for changes
  • Good encryption
  • limit the host that communicate with PoS
  • chip-card enabled PoS terminals
  • Employee screening and training
